Let the worke work of the Potter, consisting in siccity and moisture, instruct you.

Epigramme Epigram 15.


Water the Potter mixing with his clay,
Makes vessells vessels Shapd Shaped by wheele wheel without delay:
This cheifely chiefly dos does his aime aim and art expresse express,
The thirsty earth with liquor to refresh:
His road is safe, proceed, and doe do not turne turn
Drowne Drowne not your earth, nor suffer it to burne burn.
