Embleme Emblem 24. Of the Secrets of Nature.
A wolfe wolf devoured the King, and being burned restored him to life againe again.

Epigramme Epigram 24.


Take the rapacious wolfe wolf, and let him feast
His hungry stomacke stomach on the King deceasd deceased:
Let Vulcan then blow up a fire, that hee he
By th' the flames to ashes may reduced be;
Repeat this method, which at length will bring
Life and a Lyons Lions courage to the King.
