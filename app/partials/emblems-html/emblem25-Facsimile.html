Embleme Emblem 25. Of the Secrets of Nature.
The Dragon dyes dies not, except hee he be killd killed by his brother and Sister,
which are Sol and Luna.

Epigramme Epigram 25.


To kill the dragon will much art require,
Soe So sure as never after to respire:
Brother and Sister joyntly jointly must invade
His life, 'cause because nothing els else can him degrade;
Luna his sister is, who Orion Slew,
His brother Phœbus Phoebus Python overthrew.
