The same mourning, which followed the death of Osiris every year in Ægypt Egypt, was allso also celebrated after the death of Adonis in Syria and the bordering kingdoms; where for some dayes days weeping and wailing being heard, afterwards were signs of joy given, and dancing, as if hee he, that was dead, now lived anew being conveyed to heaven: From whence arose the vanity of their religion or heathenish superstition, which grew to an excessive height, the Devill Devil giving occasion, and procuring false miracles.
